# Code Of Conduct

This project is for practical, respectful collaboration on reusable agent harness skills.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, specific, and respectful.
- Discuss technical tradeoffs with evidence.
- Keep feedback focused on the contribution, not the contributor.
- Assume public comments may be read by future maintainers and users.
- Help keep examples repo-neutral and free of private details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private credentials, secrets, account details, or internal URLs.
- Repeatedly pushing product-specific workflows into reusable guidance after maintainers ask for generalization.
- Fabricating evidence, validation results, or user impact.
- Dismissing review feedback without addressing the technical concern.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this code of conduct, contact the maintainers through the repository's private contact route when available. If no private route is configured, open a minimal issue asking for a private reporting channel and do not include sensitive details.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or PRs, restrict participation, or take other moderation steps needed to protect the project.
